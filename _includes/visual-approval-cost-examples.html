Service business example

Review loops can eat a year of capacity.

780 hours

5 client-facing employees × 3 lost billable hours a week × 52 weeks.

At $150 an hour, that is about $117,000 tied up in approval laps and rework instead of client work.

Grocery example

A small structural gain can move real money.

$40K to $50K

Roughly 0.5% of annual sales in an $8 million to $10 million grocery business.

In a specialty grocery, the leak can be worse because the shopper who came for a specific item may leave and buy the basket somewhere else.
