What’s actually happening

Decisions stack up in the same place. Approvals overlap. Work gets pushed forward without clear direction just to keep things moving.

Then it comes back.

Teams fix what already moved. Leaders review things twice. Deadlines shift even when everyone is working.

That pattern doesn’t come from effort. It comes from how decisions are structured.

What this changes

This work shows where decisions actually move inside your organization using the documents you already rely on.

It identifies where approvals stack up, where authority overlaps, and where work keeps landing after each decision.

Then the structure gets redesigned so everything doesn’t keep routing through the same people, offices, or bottlenecks.

After that, ongoing support keeps it working instead of drifting back.
