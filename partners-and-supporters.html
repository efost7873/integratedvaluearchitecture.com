Partners and Supporters

Work gets stuck. It gets kicked back. The same people keep fixing it.

You’ve seen it. A plan gets approved, then sits because one person still has to sign off on everything. Work goes up for review, comes back with edits from someone who couldn’t have done it themselves, and lands right back on the same desk to be fixed again. Training gets assigned, people let the video run in the background, and nothing actually changes.

That pattern shows up everywhere. Some businesses run into it constantly and should be sending that work my way when it shows up. Some are close enough to the same problems that public affiliation makes sense now, even if a full partner relationship would be stretching it. Some people have watched this happen long enough to know it is real and want to back the work while it is still early.

Those are not the same relationship. This page gives each one a place that actually fits.

Supporter Program

That includes people around IVA, people around clients, friends, family, and anyone else who has spent enough time in real organizations to know exactly what this looks like. You have seen someone spend hours fixing an expense report because it got sent back three times over tiny edits. You have watched people sit through training just to stop the reminder emails. You have seen one reliable person quietly become the place everything lands when it goes wrong because everyone knows they will clean it up.

This is for the person who recognizes that pattern, wants to see change, and is not in a position to hire IVA as a client but still wants to back the work while it is early.
