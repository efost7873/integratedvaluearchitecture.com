Partners and Supporters

IVA's foundation

IVA is an American company focused on being part of American communities. This ideology is the foundation for our Partners Network and Supporters Programs. The people that support our work, and the communities that we support working together to achieve success.

Partners and Supporters are part of a network of complementary service providers, adjacent businesses, and individuals who believe in the value of the work we do. Curated networking designed to enhance businesses through a unified organizational design ecosystem.

Partner Network

This is for consultants, strategic planners, bookkeepers, accountants, and other businesses who operate downstream from IVA's foundational work.

Along with a public Partner Listing, members of the Partner Network are eligible for referrals, networking opportunities and a $500 credit toward fixed-fee reviews.

How you support the work

You work with clients often who don't have the capacity or structural integrity to handle the work you do. IVA is positioned to ensure that organizations landing at your doorstep are prepared for implementation.

How the work supports you

Along with ongoing referals, you get a public listing, networking opportunities, Founding Partner status if you join int he first six months, and a $500 credit if you decide to get a fixed-fee review for your own business.

Strategic Supporter

Strategic Supporters are businesses near the work and potential future clients but are not providing complementary services.

You are buying early positioning around a category that is already live, public affiliation with a new standard, and you receive a credit toward a future fixed-fee review.

How you support the work

You widen the circle around IVA in a way that is visible and credible. Your support puts us in front of owners, executives, and organizations that may not act today but will recognize the problems we solve.

How the work supports you

You receive a Public Listing with homepage exposure, early and complementary access to materials and research, networking opportunities, and a $250 credit for a fixed-fee review.

Supporter Program

Supporters are the individuals supporting the networking community we are building. This includes friends and family of our team and our clients.

We have all sat at work and watched all the problems I've described happen over and over. IVA is changing that from the ground up. If you recognize the patterns, and want to see change but aren't in a position to engage with IVA as a client, this is the program for you.

How you support the work

You give early public backing to a business that is trying to define a new standard across sectors. You help keep the work visible and ensure that your neigbors, friends, and family see the value that you see in IVA.

How the work supports you

You get visible recognition as a Supporter, founding status in the first six months, early access and supporter only access and events, and a closer line of sight into how the work is taking shape.
