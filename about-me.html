Evan Micheal Foster

Building Governance Systems That Hold Under Pressure

Evan Micheal Foster is based in Springfield, Illinois and serves as Founder and Principal Architect of Integrated Value Architecture LLC.

I build governance systems that help organizations see what their structure is doing to performance, capacity, decision-making, and long-term reliability. I created Integrated Value Architecture to address structural problems that keep returning no matter how many plans, tools, or leadership changes an organization tries.

I study how decisions move, how authority flows, and how work is shaped by the system around it. My background spans public health, scientific operations, government programs, nonprofit leadership, and organizational governance. Across all of them, I saw the same pattern. People were not failing. The structure was.

Why I Built IVA

IVA is grounded in the history of management accounting, organizational behavior, and financialization research. For more than a century, financial accounting systems shaped internal performance even though they were created for external reporting. This produced predictable distortions. IVA responds to that history by introducing a structural accounting system that reflects how modern organizations actually create value.

IVA gives leaders a clearer view of the conditions that support performance. It shows where the system creates friction, waste, delay, and burnout. It provides a structural foundation for decisions that can hold under pressure instead of collapsing back into the same internal bottlenecks.

My Background

I hold three degrees and a graduate certificate from the University of Illinois Springfield. My work spans environmental health, statewide public health programs, scientific operations, nonprofit governance, and organizational leadership. In each environment, I saw the same structural patterns that IVA now makes visible.

I also serve on the board of 350 Chicago, a nonprofit climate organization working across Chicago and Illinois. That role keeps me connected to mission-driven governance work beyond IVA and strengthens the link between my background in public health, environmental leadership, and the structural questions that led me to build this standard.

I founded Integrated Value Architecture in 2026 to formalize the system I developed and to establish IVA as a modern accounting standard for organizational governance. My work focuses on structural integrity, organizational reliability, and long-term performance. I help leaders see the structure that has been shaping their outcomes for years.
