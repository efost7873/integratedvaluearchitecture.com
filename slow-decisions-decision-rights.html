Problems | Slow Decisions

Slow decisions usually mean the call is sitting in the wrong place.

The people closest to the work already know what to do. The decision still has to climb anyway.

What actually happens

Decisions move upward. Context gets thinner. Time gets lost. Then the work comes back down with more cleanup attached.
