Evan Foster in Springfield, Illinois

Evan Foster is the founder of Integrated Value Architecture, a governance design business based in Springfield, Illinois.

What the work focuses on

Integrated Value Architecture focuses on structural overload, decision bottlenecks, governance design, and the recurring pattern where too much authority and too much work end up in the same place.

The work helps organizations identify where that pattern is showing up, then redesign how decisions, authority, and work are handled so the structure stops recreating the same strain.
