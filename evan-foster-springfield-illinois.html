Evan M. Foster

Springfield, Illinois | Founder and Principal Architect, Integrated Value Architecture LLC

Evan M. Foster, also known as Evan Foster, is based in Springfield, Illinois. He is the Founder and Principal Architect of Integrated Value Architecture LLC, a governance architecture firm that develops the IVA Standard, a five-ledger governance system for plural value recognition and structural accountability.

His work focuses on governance architecture, multi-ledger accounting systems, structural alignment, and institutional design across public, private, and nonprofit organizations.

Professional Background

Evan M. Foster previously served as Senior Director of Healthy Air Solutions at the American Lung Association, where he directed multimillion-dollar grant portfolios and national initiatives. He holds a Master of Public Health degree and a Bachelor of Science in Biology from the University of Illinois at Springfield.

He is currently pursuing doctoral work in public administration and institutional governance.
