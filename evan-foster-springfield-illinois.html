Evan M. Foster

Springfield, Illinois | Founder and Principal Architect, Integrated Value Architecture LLC

Evan M. Foster, also known as Evan Foster, is based in Springfield, Illinois. He is the founder of Integrated Value Architecture LLC.

His work focuses on why work gets stuck, why decisions drag, and why the same people keep getting buried fixing problems that should have been handled somewhere else.

Through Integrated Value Architecture, he helps organizations figure out where work and decisions are getting jammed up, then redesigns how they are handled so the same problems stop repeating.

Professional Background

Evan’s background includes finance, public health, government, and nonprofit leadership. He previously served as Senior Director of Healthy Air Solutions at the American Lung Association, where he oversaw multimillion-dollar grant portfolios and large-scale national work.

He also serves as Board Treasurer for 350 Chicago, where his role extends beyond traditional financial oversight into strategy and organizational decision-making.

He holds a Master of Public Health and a Bachelor of Science in Biology from the University of Illinois Springfield and is pursuing doctoral work in public administration.
