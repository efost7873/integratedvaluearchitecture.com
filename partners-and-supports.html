Partners and Supporters

Not every relationship with this work should start the same way.

Some businesses are already close enough to the same failures that work should move both directions. Some belong near the work publicly now, even if a full partner relationship would be a stretch today. Some people simply understand the problem, want to back the work early, and want to stay close to it while it develops.

Those are three different relationships. This page keeps them separate on purpose.

Partner Network

This is the top lane because this is where the fit is already real.

You work close enough to the same mess that referrals should actually move both directions. You may be seeing clients add staff while the work still bogs down because too many decisions run through one owner, one executive, or one office that cannot carry all of it. You may be watching good work sit still because nobody can move without one more approval. You may be seeing the same reliable person end up fixing the same category of problem over and over because the structure keeps throwing it back at them.

That is not casual overlap. That is usable alignment.

Partner is for businesses that can recognize those situations, send them my way when the problem is clearly structural, and trust that I will do the same when your work is the better fit. This only works if both sides know what they are looking at and do not waste each other’s introductions.

How you support the work

You help identify the situations where better planning, better tools, or more meetings are not fixing the real problem because authority and cleanup keep landing in the wrong places. When those cases show up in your orbit, you send them to a business built to deal with that directly.

How the work supports you

You receive a public partner listing, Founding Partner placement during the first six months, top positioning as the network grows, and a full $500 credit toward a future IVA diagnostic if direct work makes sense later.

A real example here would be an advisor, consultant, planner, or adjacent firm that keeps seeing clients hit the same wall. The plan is not the issue. The org chart is not helping. Decisions keep climbing up, cleanup keeps falling back down, and the client keeps acting like the delay is a people problem. That is the kind of handoff this lane is for.

Strategic Supporter

This lane is for businesses that belong near the work without pretending the relationship is further along than it is.

The overlap may be obvious. The public affiliation may make sense immediately. There may be a believable chance the work becomes relevant later. But if I call it a full partner relationship right now, I am stretching the category just to make the list longer, and that kind of fake neatness ruins pages like this.

Strategic Supporter gives a business a credible early connection without forcing a two-way referral promise that has not been earned yet.

This is for the firm that keeps brushing up against structural strain, decision drag, overloaded leadership, or repeated execution problems and knows the connection is real, even if the business relationship is still developing.

How you support the work

You widen the visible business circle around IVA, increase public credibility for the work, and help put it in front of organizations that may not be ready now but are likely to recognize the problem when it becomes impossible to ignore.

How the work supports you

You receive a public business listing, Founding Strategic Supporter placement during the first six months, control over how your business appears on the site, and a $250 credit toward a future IVA diagnostic if the fit becomes active later.

A real example here would be a business that works near leadership friction, operational slowdowns, or organizational coordination problems and wants to be publicly connected to this work now, without claiming a standing referral relationship before one actually exists.

Supporter Program

This one is for people who want to back the work publicly and stay close to it while it grows.

They are not joining because they need a business development credit. They are joining because they know this pattern when they see it. They have watched one competent person quietly become the cleanup crew for everybody else. They have sat through useless training just to stop the reminder emails. They have seen work go up the chain for approval, come back with edits from someone who cannot do the work, and land right back on the desk of the person who already did it once.

Supporter is for people who want their name attached to the work while it is still early and who want a closer line of sight into what is being built, written, tested, and put into the world.

That means this lane has to offer more than a thank-you and a listing. The value here is early visibility, founding recognition, and real proximity to the work as it develops.

How you support the work

You provide early public backing, visible legitimacy, and proof that this work is landing with people who have lived close enough to these failures to recognize them immediately.

How the work supports you

You receive public recognition on the site, Founding Supporter placement during the first six months, top positioning as the supporter network grows, and closer connection to the work through early visibility into what is taking shape before it reaches everyone else.

A real example here would be someone who has spent years watching good people get buried under cleanup, delay, and repeated rework and wants to publicly back a business that is finally naming the problem for what it is instead of dressing it up.
