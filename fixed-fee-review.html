Fixed-Fee Review

The fastest way to see where decisions are slowing you down.

This is where most organizations start.

You don’t need to build anything new or prepare anything special. The review uses the documents you already rely on to run your organization.

That usually includes reporting lines, policies, workflows, committee structures, operating materials, and planning documents.

Those documents already show how decisions are handled. They just haven’t been mapped this way before.

What happens during the review

The review maps how decisions actually move across your organization.

It shows where approvals stack up, where authority overlaps, and where work gets pushed downstream after each decision.

It also shows where the same problems keep coming back and why.

What you get

You get a clear view of where decisions are slowing things down and what needs to change to fix it.

You see where work is landing, who is carrying it, and why it keeps coming back.

You also get a path forward, whether that means making targeted changes or moving into deeper work.

What this is not

This is not a generic assessment, a process map, or a report that sits on a shelf.

It connects directly to how your organization is already operating and shows where the structure is creating the problem.

The goal is to make the issue visible and actionable as quickly as possible.
