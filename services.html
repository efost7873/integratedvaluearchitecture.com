Services | Integrated Value Architecture

IVA Offers:

Now that you see the problem, it's time to choose where to start. Each solution is custom-built for your organization from advisory calls, to multi-ledger governance redesign through partner networks with mutual benefits. Each organization we work with is unique, with a unique structure, and deserves a unique solution.

Quick solutions

These services are designed to identify structural problems quickly when time is limited. A paid advisory call offer 60-minutes of intense structural mapping of a single problem so you leave knowing where the underlying issue is. Targeted reviews are extended engagements over several days to identify issues or second-opinions on strategic plans, scorecard or consulting services. Fixed-fee reviews are several weeks of intense document and structural mapping to identify all structural issues and provide you with a strucural mapping aligned with a multi-ledger implementation.

Paid advisory call

60-minute solution for acute issues requiring a rapid fix.

Targeted review

An thorough review of a single issue with a maximum of 6-hours of review, analysis and presentation.

Fixed-fee review

A custom diagnostic of the full organizational structure with issue mapping, multi-ledger opportunities, decision-mapping, and comprehensive structural blueprint.

What our review includes

Each review consists of data collection, mapping, and presentation. Final reports provide you with a clear picture of the issue(s) and how the structure of your organization contributes to these issues.

Advisory calls are priced for rapid corrective action. Targeted reviews are priced at an hourly rate up to 6-hours and include a thorough document evaluation. Fixed-fee reviews are organization/department wide comprehensive reviews with extended engagements priced according to the IVA Pricing Tree.

Build on the review

Reviews provide a cross-sectional picture of your organization. IVA supports work to correct issues independently. However, sometimes seeing the full picture makes it clear that a redesign provides the greatest return on investment.

We offer two paths for extended engagements. The first is our signature path which is the IVA Multi-Ledger Implementation. This is a full organizational redesign from the ground up. The second path is through ongoing support as your organization implements changes.

Implementation

Comprehesive multi-ledger organizational redesign implemented over several months of continuous engagement. Ensures ongoing alignment with your organizations long-term expectations.

Ongoing support

Retainer-based structural support to address long-standing or recurring issues on an as-needed basis.
