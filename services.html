Services | Integrated Value Architecture

IVA Offers:

Now that you see the problem, it's time to choose where to start. Each solution is custom-built for your organization from advisory calls, to multi-ledger governance redesign through partner networks with mutual benefits. Each organization we work with is unique, with a unique structure, and deserves a unique solution.

Quick solutions

These services are designed to identify structural problems quickly when time is limited. A paid advisory call offer 60-minutes of intense structural mapping of a single problem so you leave knowing where the underlying issue is. Targeted reviews are extended engagements over several days to identify issues or second-opinions on strategic plans, scorecard or consulting services. Fixed-fee reviews are several weeks of intense document and structural mapping to identify all structural issues and provide you with a strucural mapping aligned with a multi-ledger implementation.

Paid advisory call

60-minute solution for acute issues requiring a rapid fix.

Targeted review

An thorough review of a single issue with a maximum of 6-hours of review, analysis and presentation.

Fixed-fee review

A custom diagnostic of the full organizational structure with issue mapping, multi-ledger opportunities, decision-mapping, and comprehensive structural blueprint.

What I actually do in the review stage

I work through the documents you already use to run the place. Budgets, org charts, reporting, policy language, board material. Then I trace where decisions sit, where they stall, and where work gets sent back or rerouted.

If you want to see what comes out of that, take a look at what clients get or the common problems this work catches.

What happens if the issue goes deeper

Sometimes the review is enough. Other times it shows that the problem is built into where decisions and work are sitting. That is when the work moves into implementation.

Implementation

Decision rights shift. Work stops climbing to the wrong place. Fewer returns, fewer fixes, less wasted time.

Ongoing support

Keeps the same problems from rebuilding once pressure starts to rise again.

What this is not

This is not project management. Not workflow cleanup. Not another layer of reporting. If you need better tracking or tools, there are people who do that well.

This work focuses on where decisions sit, where work lands, and why the same problems keep coming back. If you want the underlying structure, you can read the IVA Standard.
