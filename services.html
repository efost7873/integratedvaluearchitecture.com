What happens during review

Each review consists of data collection, mapping, and presentation. Final reports provide you with a clear picture of the issue(s) and how the structure of your organization contributes to these issues.

Advisory calls are priced for rapid corrective action. Targeted reviews are billed hourly up to 6-hours and include a thorough document evaluation. Fixed-fee reviews are organization/department wide comprehensive reviews with extended engagements priced according to the IVA Pricing Tree.
