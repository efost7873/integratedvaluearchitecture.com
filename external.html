Externalities and Equity Ledger

Organizations affect more than what shows up on internal reports

Decisions can look efficient inside the organization and still shift cost, harm, delay, or inequity somewhere else. When that impact has no standing, it gets ignored until it creates a bigger problem.

What this ledger tracks

It tracks external impact, equity effects, and obligations that do not disappear just because they sit outside the internal reporting frame. That includes consequences for communities, stakeholders, service recipients, and others carrying the cost of internal decisions.

Why it matters structurally

When external impact has no real standing, organizations keep making decisions that look clean internally while shifting pressure outward. That weakens trust, creates avoidable harm, and often comes back as operational or legal strain later.

IVA vs GRC Good Governance

How it connects to the rest of the system

External impact is not separate from operations, capacity, or finance. It is often the result of decisions made elsewhere in the structure. This ledger keeps those consequences visible instead of letting them disappear into someone else’s problem.
